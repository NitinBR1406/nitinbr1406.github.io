Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Unchained Nitin Publisher" application ("the App") handles information when it is used to publish video content to TikTok on behalf of the artist Unchained Nitin. The App is a personal automation tool used exclusively by Unchained Nitin and does not collect data from third-party users.

2. Who We Are

Unchained Nitin
Email: nitin_ramdaras@outlook.com

3. Data We Collect

The App does not collect personal data from TikTok users or the general public. The only data processed is video content created by Unchained Nitin and TikTok API credentials used to authenticate with TikTok on behalf of the account owner.

4. How We Use the TikTok API

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API with the video.publish and video.upload scopes to upload and publish video content to the TikTok account of Unchained Nitin. No data is stored beyond what is necessary to complete the publishing action.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Video content is transmitted directly to TikTok in accordance with TikTok's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

The App does not retain personal data. Access tokens are stored only as long as necessary to perform the publishing action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
